## Microsoft 365 Copilot is only as good as your data [.text-h4]

Microsoft 365 Copilot is one of the most powerful productivity tools available to small businesses right now. But it surfaces whatever is in your environment — including files that shouldn't be widely accessible, outdated documents, and disorganised SharePoint sites.

The AI Workplace Readiness package assesses your current environment, identifies what needs to change, and builds a practical adoption roadmap — so when you turn on Copilot, it works for you, not against you.

## What's included [.text-h4]

Every AI Workplace Readiness engagement includes:

- Microsoft 365 licence and feature availability review (which Copilot capabilities your plan includes)
- SharePoint information architecture assessment — site structure, permissions, content quality
- Oversharing and permissions audit — identifying content accessible to everyone that shouldn't be
- Data governance review — retention policies, sensitivity labels, DLP status
- Microsoft Copilot readiness scorecard with gap analysis
- Prioritised remediation plan (what to fix before enabling Copilot)
- AI adoption roadmap with role-based use cases for your business
- Recommended training resources for your team

This package is best suited for businesses already on Microsoft 365 Business Standard or higher, considering a Microsoft 365 Copilot licence.
