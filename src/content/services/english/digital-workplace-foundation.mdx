## Everything your team needs to work productively [.text-h4]

The Digital Workplace Foundation package sets up your Microsoft 365 environment correctly from the start. Whether you're moving from an old email system, adopting Teams for the first time, or simply want to make sure your existing setup is done properly — this is where we begin.

We configure your tenant, migrate your email and data, set up collaboration tools, and ensure every user has the access they need — with MFA enforced from day one.

## What's included [.text-h4]

Every Digital Workplace Foundation engagement includes:

- Microsoft 365 tenant configuration and verified domain setup
- Exchange Online email migration (from Google Workspace, on-premises, or other providers)
- Microsoft Teams structure aligned to your team and projects
- SharePoint and OneDrive setup for document storage and sharing
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enforced for all users
- User onboarding guide and short training session
- 30-day post-implementation support

This package is designed for businesses of 5–25 people that are new to Microsoft 365 or want to ensure their existing setup is done properly.
