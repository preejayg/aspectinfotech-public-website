# Target Customer Profile

## Geography

- Australia
- Businesses with an ABN

## Company Size

- 5 to 25 users

## Ideal Customer

Businesses that:

- Have no dedicated IT team
- Use Microsoft 365 or are considering Microsoft 365
- Need better collaboration and document management
- Need improved security and device management
- Are interested in Microsoft Copilot and AI productivity

## Typical Industries

- Professional services
- Accounting firms
- Consulting firms
- Trades businesses
- Real estate agencies
- Small healthcare practices
- Other small businesses

## Common Business Challenges

- Email and files are not organised
- No document management standards
- Weak security controls
- No device management
- Inconsistent onboarding processes
- No clear AI adoption strategy

## Not a Good Fit

- Large enterprises
- Government agencies
- Businesses requiring extensive ongoing support
- Complex hybrid infrastructure environments
- Businesses larger than 25 users (initially)
