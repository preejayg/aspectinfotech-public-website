# Positioning

## Brand

Aspect Infotech

## What We Do

Aspect Infotech helps small businesses establish secure, productive and modern digital workplaces.

We focus on implementing the technology foundations that enable businesses to communicate, collaborate, manage information securely and prepare for AI-driven productivity.

Our preferred platform is Microsoft 365, including Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive, Intune, Entra ID and Microsoft Copilot.

## What We Deliver

- Business email and communications
- Team collaboration
- Document management
- Device management
- Identity and security
- AI productivity readiness
- Business process enablement

## What Makes Us Different

Most providers focus on selling software or providing IT support.

We focus on designing and implementing a complete digital workplace that enables businesses to work securely, efficiently and consistently.

## What We Are Not

- General IT helpdesk
- Printer support
- Hardware repair services
- Unlimited on-demand support
- Managed service provider (MSP)

We focus on implementation, modernization, security and advisory engagements.
