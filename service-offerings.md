# Service Offerings

## Offer 1: Digital Workplace Foundation

### Outcome

Establish a secure and productive digital workplace foundation.

### Includes

- Microsoft 365 tenant setup
- Business email setup
- Teams setup
- SharePoint setup
- OneDrive setup
- User onboarding
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Basic documentation

### Ideal For

- New businesses
- Businesses moving to Microsoft 365
- Businesses with fragmented systems

---

## Offer 2: Secure Workplace

### Outcome

Improve security, governance and device management.

### Includes

- Identity review
- MFA assessment
- Intune setup
- Device compliance policies
- Conditional access recommendations
- Security baseline implementation

### Ideal For

- Existing Microsoft 365 customers
- Businesses with unmanaged devices
- Businesses concerned about cyber security

---

## Offer 3: AI Workplace Readiness

### Outcome

Prepare the business for successful AI adoption.

### Includes

- Microsoft 365 assessment
- SharePoint review
- Permissions review
- Information architecture review
- Copilot readiness assessment
- AI adoption roadmap

### Ideal For

- Businesses evaluating Microsoft Copilot
- Businesses seeking productivity improvements
- Businesses wanting AI readiness guidance
