# Digital Workplace Assessment

## Purpose

Provide small businesses with a free, high-level assessment of their current digital workplace and identify opportunities for improvement.

## Assessment Areas

### 1. Email & Identity

Review:

- Business email platform
- User account management
- Identity management
- Multi-factor authentication

### 2. Collaboration

Review:

- Teams usage
- Internal communication
- Meeting capabilities
- Collaboration practices

### 3. Document Management

Review:

- File storage
- File sharing
- Version control
- Document organisation

### 4. Device Management

Review:

- Company devices
- Mobile devices
- Device security
- Device lifecycle management

### 5. Security

Review:

- MFA
- Identity protection
- Device protection
- Basic security controls

### 6. AI Readiness

Review:

- Microsoft 365 maturity
- Information organisation
- Permission structures
- Copilot readiness

## Assessment Output

### Scorecard

Each area is rated:

- Green
- Amber
- Red

### Recommendations

- Three immediate improvements
- Three medium-term improvements
- Suggested implementation roadmap

## Objective

The assessment is designed to identify opportunities for Digital Workplace Foundation, Secure Workplace and AI Workplace Readiness engagements.
